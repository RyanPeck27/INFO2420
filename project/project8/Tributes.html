Pet Tributes

At Rent-A-Pet, we honor the special animals who have brought happiness to families across our community. These tributes celebrate their stories and the joy they shared with others.

Daisy

Daisy was a gentle and curious little hamster who became a favorite among children visiting our weekend events. Her calm personality and big bright eyes made her a cherished companion. Daisy helped many families discover the joys of small-pet care.

Max

Max was a rescued horse known for his calm and friendly nature. He participated in therapy sessions, helping individuals overcome anxiety and develop confidence.

Hunter & Scout

Hunter and Scout were energetic and loyal dogs who loved outdoor activities. Their playful personalities made them popular choices for families wanting a full-day adventure companion.
